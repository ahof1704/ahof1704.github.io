About

Hello! I am Antonio Fonseca. Engineer, Neuroscientist and Rock Climber.

I am a Neuroscience Ph.D student working at the intersection between Computational Neuroscience and Machine Learning. Currently, I am interested in using Deep Learning to model brain dynamics in mesoscopic calcium imaging data and what it can tells us about cognition and behavior. I am advised by David Van Dijk and working closely to Jess Cardin. Previously, I worked on software development for mining robots and solutions for banking companies. Most recently, I worked on applying machine learning and signal processing techniques to investigate behavior in developing animals at the Dietrich Lab at Yale.
